Introduction

“Creation of demand for welfare and establishment of a healthy living environment”

  • The Advisory Office for Health and Welfare is responsible for establishing customized-integrated welfare and a warm-hearted welfare safety net, supporting policies for the disabled and elderly people, establishing health promotion services and safe health and medical systems, and creating a safe food environment.

Jurisdiction: Health and Welfare Bureau, Female Welfare Office (Social Welfare Officer, Health and Hygiene Officer), Gyeonggi Institute of Health & Environment (partly), Gyeonggi Provincial Medical Center, Gyeonggi Welfare Foundation
